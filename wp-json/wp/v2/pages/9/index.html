Principal’s Desk<\/h3>\n

Education is about awakening \u2013Awakening to the power and beauty that lies within all of us.<\/p>\n

To my mind education as an idea, is not just about bricks ,mortar and concrete , but about building character , enriching minds and about varied experiences that last a lifetime.<\/p>\n

AOL\u2019s vision is to give the students the all round capability including creativity ,observation and knowledge empowerment leading to the generation of excellent ,performing citizens with sterling character.<\/p>\n

We, at AOL , practice RICRAC behavior. The RICRAC acronym is used to remind students & parents \u201cResponsibility , Integrity, Caring , Respectful , Academic and Cooperative\u201d. These values , we believe , will enable our students to take a leadership role in learning and in life.<\/p>\n

There is a commitment to an integrated curriculum approach through inquiry learning. The development of higher order thinking skills, risk taking ,adaptability, cooperation, and communication techniques are fostered.<\/p>\n
